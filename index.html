About RefugeeConnect

RefugeeConnect was established to address the growing need for effective solutions in managing refugee support operations. Our platform equips organizations with tools to streamline communication, track resources, and deliver aid efficiently.

With a dedicated team of developers, humanitarians, and technologists, we have empowered over 100 global organizations, providing critical support to those in need.

Our Mission

At RefugeeConnect, we aim to revolutionize refugee support by providing humanitarian organizations with tools to enhance efficiency, coordination, and impact.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy: Always putting people first in everything we do.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with organizations for maximum impact.
  • Innovation: Using technology to solve the toughest challenges.
  • Reliability: Building trust through secure, user-friendly solutions.

Our Journey

  • 2019: RefugeeConnect was founded.
  • 2020: Launched our first platform for resource tracking.
  • 2022: Partnered with 50+ global NGOs.
  • 2023: Expanded to serve refugees in 20 countries.

Our Platform

RefugeeConnect offers a secure, cloud-based platform designed to simplify communication, document management, and resource tracking for organizations aiding refugees.

How We Help

Here’s how organizations use RefugeeConnect to make a difference:

  • Coordinating disaster relief efforts in real time.
